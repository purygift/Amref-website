About Us

Best Medical Care For Yourself and Your Family

Welcome to the Kibera Community Health Center, where we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of individuals within our community. At Kibera CHC, we are more than just a health center; we are a dedicated team committed to providing comprehensive support for all health matters effectively. With a deep-rooted belief in the well-being of our community, we strive to be an effective hub for healthcare, ensuring that every individual receives the care and attention they deserve. From preventive services to treatment and ongoing support, we are here for you every step of the way.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to promote health and wellness while addressing the unique needs of our community. We understand that good health is essential for a vibrant community, and that's why we offer a range of services aimed at improving the overall health outcomes of individuals and families in Kibera. At Kibera CHC, you'll find a compassionate team of healthcare professionals who are dedicated to your well-being. Together, we work tirelessly to create a healthier, happier community for all. Your health is our priority, and we're here to support you on your journey to a better quality of life.

Services

Excellent Medical Services

Testing

At Kibera Community Health Center, we offer comprehensive testing services for various conditions, including HIV, tuberculosis, and malaria. Our routine screenings ensure early detection and effective health management. We empower our community with accessible and reliable health testing.

Counseling

At Kibera Community Health Center, we offer specialized counseling for HIV patients to support their emotional well-being, manage stigma, and adhere to treatment plans. Our services include personalized therapy and support groups, aiming to empower patients and improve their quality of life.

Child Welfare Services

At Kibera Community Health Center, Child Welfare Services offer immunizations, nutritional support, check-ups, and developmental screenings for children. We provide parent education and support for vulnerable children, aiming to create a nurturing environment for every child to thrive.

Medicine & Pharmacy

At Kibera Community Health Center, our pharmacy provides essential medications and personalized counseling for safe and effective use. We are committed to supporting the health and well-being of our community through quality pharmaceutical services.

Blood Testing

At Kibera Community Health Center, we offer comprehensive blood testing services for various health conditions, aiding in early detection and monitoring. With our state-of-the-art laboratory and skilled professionals, we ensure accurate diagnostics for the well-being of our community.

Mission, Vision, and Core Values

Mission:

Our mission is to provide accessible, compassionate, and quality healthcare services to the community of Kibera, promoting health and well-being for all.

Vision:

Our vision is to be a leading healthcare center dedicated to transforming lives and empowering our community to thrive in health and wellness.

Core Values:

  • Compassion: We are committed to providing care with empathy and understanding.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our services, continually seeking improvement.
  • Community: We prioritize the well-being of our community and actively engage in its health promotion.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards, honesty, and transparency in all our actions.
  • Teamwork: We collaborate and support each other to achieve our common goals.
  • Inclusivity: We respect diversity and ensure equal access to healthcare for all.
