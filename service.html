Our Nutrition department at Kibera Community Health Center also ensures access to essential immunizations for all ages. We prioritize preventive care to safeguard the health of our community.

At our Comprehensive Care Clinic, we provide holistic support for HIV and TB patients, ensuring comprehensive treatment and care. With a dedicated team and personalized services, we strive to improve health outcomes and well-being.

Outpatient Services

Our Outpatient Services at Kibera Community Health Center offer prompt and quality care for various medical needs. With compassionate support and efficient treatments, we prioritize your health and convenience.

At Kibera Community Health Center, our Maternity services provide comprehensive care for expectant mothers throughout their journey. From prenatal care to postnatal support, we ensure the well-being of both mother and baby.

At Kibera Community Health Center, our Pharmacy offers a wide range of essential medications and expert counseling for safe and effective use. With compassionate care and accessibility as our priorities, we strive to support your health needs.
