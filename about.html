About Us

Best Medical Care For Yourself and Your Family

Welcome to the Kibera Community Health Center, where we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of individuals within our community. At Kibera CHC, we are more than just a health center; we are a dedicated team committed to providing comprehensive support for all health matters effectively. With a deep-rooted belief in the well-being of our community, we strive to be an effective hub for healthcare, ensuring that every individual receives the care and attention they deserve. From preventive services to treatment and ongoing support, we are here for you every step of the way.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to promote health and wellness while addressing the unique needs of our community. We understand that good health is essential for a vibrant community, and that's why we offer a range of services aimed at improving the overall health outcomes of individuals and families in Kibera. At Kibera CHC, you'll find a compassionate team of healthcare professionals who are dedicated to your well-being. Together, we work tirelessly to create a healthier, happier community for all. Your health is our priority, and we're here to support you on your journey to a better quality of life.

We invite donors and supporters to join us in this crucial mission. By contributing to Kibera Community Health Center, you are not only helping us provide immediate relief and vital services but also investing in the long-term well-being and prosperity of the Kibera community. Your generosity can create a ripple effect of positive change and hope for thousands of lives.

Our Departments

Comprehesive Care Clinic(CCC)

At the Kibera Community Health Center, our Comprehensive Care Unit (CCC) is dedicated to supporting HIV and TB patients with specialized, compassionate care. Our expert team provides comprehensive treatment, counseling, and support services to manage and improve health outcomes.

Nutrition Department

At Kibera Community Health Center, our Nutrition Department plays a crucial role in promoting health through our comprehensive immunization program. Our dedicated team ensures that every child receives essential vaccines, safeguarding them against preventable diseases. We are committed to nurturing a healthier future for our community, one immunization at a time.

Pharmacy

At Kibera Community Health Center, our Pharmacy Services ensure that you have access to essential medications and expert pharmaceutical care. Our dedicated pharmacists are here to provide guidance, answer your questions, and support your treatment plans. We are committed to enhancing your health and well-being with reliable and efficient service.

Mother Child Clinic(MCH)

At our Mother Child Clinic (MCH), we provide comprehensive care for mothers and children, including antenatal and postnatal clinics, family planning, and cervical cancer screening. Our dedicated team offers personalized support at every stage of motherhood, ensuring the well-being of both mother and child. Trust us for compassionate care and essential services to promote the health of you and your family.

Lab Services

At Kibera Community Health Center, our Lab Services offer accurate and timely diagnostic testing to support your healthcare journey. Our state-of-the-art facilities and skilled technicians ensure reliable results for a range of medical tests. Trust us to provide precise and comprehensive lab services, empowering you to make informed decisions about your health.

Outpatients Services

At Kibera Community Health Center, our Outpatient Services provide accessible, high-quality medical care for all your health needs. Our dedicated healthcare professionals offer a wide range of services, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments, ensuring you receive timely and effective care. We are committed to supporting your well-being with compassionate and personalized outpatient care.

Marternity

Our Maternity Services at Kibera Community Health Center provide comprehensive care for expecting mothers, ensuring a safe and healthy journey from pregnancy to childbirth.
Our skilled and compassionate team is dedicated to offering personalized support, prenatal care, and educational resources to empower mothers throughout their pregnancy.
